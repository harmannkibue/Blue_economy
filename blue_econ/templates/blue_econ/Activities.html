Do not miss Nor-Shipping Blue Economy Hall

Date : 04-07-19

Do not miss the World Ocean Summit 2019:

5th - 7th/03/19


Activities will be updated regularly..


Different countries in the world all year round hold different events that are geared to growing and networking the Blue Economy community.
In these events you will be able to learn about:

  1. The latest policing in financing the initiative.

  2. The impact of women in the initiative and how we can better recognise their efforts.

  3. The role of the local community in the growth and continued development of the initiative.

  4. Developing better initiatives so as to reach more people supporting the initiative.

  5. Measures being developed to curtail the continued disruption of marine life.

  6. How to network with different organizations and people so as to faster reach the Blue Economy goal.
